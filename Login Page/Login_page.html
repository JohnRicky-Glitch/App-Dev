About Us

Welcome to Barangay San Roque, a vibrant community nestled in the heart of Quezon City, Metro Manila, home to over 18,000 residents. Our lively streets and warm, welcoming atmosphere provide an authentic glimpse into local life, making San Roque a unique destination for both residents and visitors alike.

Established through Executive Order No. 052 during the administration of Mayor Norberto S. Amoranto, Barangay San Roque has been a cornerstone of community engagement since the inauguration of its barangay hall in 1981. This facility serves as a hub for local activities and services, ensuring that our community remains connected and engaged. Our barangay office is open for your convenience from Monday to Saturday, 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM.

At Juan of Us, we are proud to offer two essential services designed to enhance your experience within our community. First, we facilitate the reservation of equipment and materials, allowing you to check real-time availability and easily apply for what you need. You can schedule your borrowing in advance to ensure a seamless delivery experience.

Additionally, we provide an appointment system for document requests, designed to streamline your visit and reduce wait times. By scheduling an appointment, you can process your documents with ease, ensuring a smoother and more efficient experience.

Our Mission

Our mission is to foster community engagement and improve the overall quality of life for all residents. We aim to provide services that enhance accessibility, promote unity, and encourage active participation in both local and citywide initiatives. Through innovative solutions and collaborative efforts, we strive to create a supportive environment where individuals can thrive and work together toward common goals.

Our Vision

We envision a vibrant and inclusive Barangay San Roque where every resident feels connected, valued, and empowered. By leveraging technology and fostering a culture of collaboration, we aspire to build a community that is resilient, forward-thinking, and united in its efforts to improve the well-being of all. Our ultimate goal is to create a safe, sustainable, and prosperous future for generations to come.

Employment and Training Programs

Barangay San Roque offers employment and training programs aimed at enhancing the skills of residents, providing opportunities for career growth, and supporting job placement to improve livelihoods.

Free Use of Barangay Facilities and Properties

Residents of Barangay San Roque are entitled to the free use of community facilities and properties, such as event spaces, tents, and tables, for personal or community events.

Issuance of Barangay Certificates and Clearances

Barangay San Roque provides residents with official certificates and clearances, such as Barangay Clearance, Barangay ID, and business permits, which are essential for various legal, employment, and business purposes.

Health Services for Pets (Dogs and Cats)

Barangay San Roque offers free health services for pets, including anti-rabies vaccination, deworming, and microchipping, conducted every six months to ensure the well-being of both pets and the community.

Barangay Day Care Center (Free Services)

The Barangay San Roque Day Care Center offers free early childhood education and care services for young children, providing a safe and nurturing environment that supports their development while helping parents manage their daily responsibilities.
